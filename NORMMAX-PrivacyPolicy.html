NORMMAX Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NORMMAX. We take your privacy seriously. NORMMAX is a local utility for normal distribution calculations, including PDF, CDF, tail probabilities, Z-scores, inverse quantiles, percentiles, confidence intervals, and related statistical tools. Before using the app, please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By tapping "I Agree", you confirm that you have read and accepted this policy.

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. NORMMAX does not provide financial advice, trading services, brokerage, or investment recommendations.

2. Information We Collect

NORMMAX works on your device and does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information from you over the internet. The app may store the following information locally on your device:

Calculation parameters you enter, such as mean (μ), standard deviation (σ), values (x), probabilities, and mode-specific inputs; calculation results and labels produced on your device; saved calculation history records, including timestamps when you choose to save results; app preferences, such as decimal precision, auto-evaluate, auto-save, chart display options, default parameters, and privacy consent status.

We do not collect payment information, government ID numbers, precise location data, contacts, photos, or device advertising identifiers for tracking purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use information stored on your device solely to provide and improve NORMMAX features, including performing statistical calculations, displaying curve previews, maintaining your local history list, showing summary statistics, filtering and sorting saved records, and exporting your history when you choose to export. We do not sell your personal information or use it for purposes unrelated to the app without your consent.

4. Storage and Protection

Your data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage mechanisms, including the app sandbox and local files managed by NORMMAX. Calculation history and preferences remain on your device until you delete individual records, clear all history from Settings, export and remove copies yourself, or uninstall NORMMAX.

We take reasonable measures to help protect information on your device. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure. Please protect your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not transmit your calculation inputs, results, or history to our servers. We may share information only when you have given explicit consent, such as when you export JSON or CSV files and choose where to save or send them; when required by law or a competent authority; or when necessary to protect the legitimate rights and interests of you, us, or other users.

When you first launch the app, it may load this Privacy Policy over the internet.

6. Your Rights

You may export your calculation history as JSON or CSV from Settings; delete individual history records in the History tab; clear all local history from Settings; adjust or reset app preferences within the app; and uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device. To ask questions or request assistance, please contact us as described in Section 9.

7. Protection of Minors

NORMMAX is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a minor, please read this policy with your guardian and use the app only with their consent. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of NORMMAX after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or NORMMAX, please contact us at: NORMMAX2026@outlook.com
